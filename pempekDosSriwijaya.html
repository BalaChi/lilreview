Traditional Food = Awesome Thing to be Tasted

Article by AD Zone Editorial Jan 11 2024

Moving to Jakarta several years ago made think about starting her own business and chose to develop a cooking activity that would not only become a routine but also provide additional income. According to the owner of , culinary delights are always fun to explore, create, and of course taste. Therefore, Novi decided to focus on the traditional food business of kambu, which is her birthplace, namely pempek.

Idli, a staple of Tamil Nadu, is a steamed rice cake made from fermented batter. Served with sambar and chutney, it's nutritious and delicious. Idli is soft, fluffy, and pairs perfectly with the spicy tang of sambar.

Kambu, also known as pearl millet, is a staple in Tamil Nadu cuisine, often used to make nutritious dishes. It's ground into flour for dosas, rotis, and porridge. Kambu is rich in fiber, protein

Tamil Nadu's cuisine is renowned for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and diverse range of dishes. Staple ingredients like rice, lentils.
